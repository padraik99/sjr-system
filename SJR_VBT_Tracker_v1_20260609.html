How it reads: each upward bar movement past the minimum ROM logs a rep with its mean concentric velocity (displacement ÷ time — robust at 30fps). High beep = within 5% of your session best. Low double-buzz = velocity loss past 20% — terminate the set (Pareja-Blanco 2017: more fatigue, no extra gain). Best rep auto-sends to the guide's readiness panel. Set resets after 2 min of stillness.
